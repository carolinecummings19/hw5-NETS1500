Ingredients:
- cooking spray
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 ¼ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup banana, mashed
- ¼ cup applesauce
- 2 egg whites

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
- Stir flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Add banana, applesauce, and egg whites; stir just until combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 50 to 55 minutes. Turn out onto a wire rack and allow to cool before slicing. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

127 Calories 0g Fat 30g Carbs 2g Protein
