Ingredients:
- 1 (3 pound) whole chicken
- 1 large onion, halved - unpeeled
- 3 carrots, cut into chunks - unpeeled
- 2 stalks celery, cut into chunks
- 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place chicken in a large pot with onion, carrots, celery, and peppercorns; add water to cover by 1 inch. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover the pot and bring to a boil; reduce heat to a gentle boil and cook until meat falls off the bone, about 90 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove chicken from the pot and let sit until cool enough to handle. Shred or chop meat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Nutrition Facts:
186 Calories 11g Fat 5g Carbs 16g Protein
