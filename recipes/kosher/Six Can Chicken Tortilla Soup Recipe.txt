Ingredients:
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
- 1 (10 ounce) can chunk chicken
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, drained
- crushed tortilla chips (Optional)
- shredded Cheddar cheese (Optional)

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour chicken broth, corn, black beans, chunk chicken, and diced tomatoes with green chilies into a large saucepan or stockpot over medium heat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Simmer, stirring occasionally, until chicken is heated through, about 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve hot, topped with tortilla chips and grated cheese.

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 20 mins
Servings: 6
