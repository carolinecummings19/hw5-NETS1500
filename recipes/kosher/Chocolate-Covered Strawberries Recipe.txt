Ingredients:
- 16 ounces milk chocolate chips
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- toothpicks
- 1 pound fresh strawberries with leaves

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Will Dickey / Food Styling: Ana Kelly / Prop Styling: Julia Bayless
- Melt chocolate and shortening in a double boiler, stirring occasionally until smooth. Will Dickey / Food Styling: Ana Kelly / Prop Styling: Julia Bayless
- Insert toothpicks into tops (stem ends) of strawberries. Hold a strawberry by the toothpick and dip it into the chocolate mixture. Will Dickey / Food Styling: Ana Kelly / Prop Styling: Julia Bayless
- Turn the strawberry upside down and insert the toothpick into a Styrofoam block or place strawberry on a sheet of waxed paper; allow chocolate coating to cool. Repeat with remaining strawberries. Will Dickey / Food Styling: Ana Kelly / Prop Styling: Julia Bayless
- Enjoy! Will Dickey / Food Styling: Ana Kelly / Prop Styling: Julia Bayless
