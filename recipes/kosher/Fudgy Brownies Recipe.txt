Ingredients:
- ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ⅔ cup vegetable oil, divided
- ½ cup boiling water
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir cocoa and baking soda together in a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix in boiling water and 1/3 cup oil until well blended and thickened. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in sugar, remaining 1/3 cup oil, and eggs. Mix in flour, vanilla, and salt just until flour is absorbed. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spread evenly into the prepared pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Let cool completely before cutting into 24 squares. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
