Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup cold water
- ½ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
- Combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl; mix well using a fork. Stir in oil, vinegar, and vanilla until thoroughly moistened. Pour in cold water and mix until batter is smooth. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
- Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.

Nutrition Facts:
319 Calories 12g Fat 51g Carbs 4g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 1 (8x8-inch) cake
