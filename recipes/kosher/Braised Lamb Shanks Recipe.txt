Ingredients:
- 6 lamb shanks
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 large carrots, cut into ¼-inch rounds
- 2 onions, chopped
- 10 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (750 milliliter) bottle red wine
- 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes with juice
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
- 5 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Season lamb shanks with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat oil in a large, heavy pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Working in batches, cook lamb shanks until brown on all sides, about 8 minutes. Transfer shanks to a plate; set aside. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add carrots, onions, and garlic to the pot and sauté over medium heat until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in wine, tomatoes, chicken broth, beef broth, rosemary, and thyme. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Return lamb shanks to the pot, pressing down to submerge. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer until lamb is tender, about 2 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove the lid and simmer to allow flavors to concentrate, about 20 minutes. Transfer lamb shanks to a platter; cover with foil to keep warm. Continue boiling sauce until thickened, about 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spoon sauce over lamb shanks before serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
