Ingredients:
- 1 (5½ pound) boneless leg of lamb, tied in netting
- 20 cloves garlic, peeled, or more to taste
- garlic powder, or to taste
- 10 fresh rosemary sprigs

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Allrecipes / Christine Ma
- Cut 20 deep slits evenly spaced around the leg of lamb without cutting the netting. Stuff one garlic clove into each slit. Allrecipes / Christine Ma
- Rub garlic powder over lamb. Slice rosemary sprigs; place under the netting evenly around lamb. Transfer lamb to a roasting pan. Allrecipes / Christine Ma
- Roast lamb in the preheated oven until pink and juicy in the center for medium, about 2 to 2 ½ hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 130 degrees F (54 degrees C). Allrecipes / Christine Ma
- Remove from the oven and cover with aluminum foil; lest rest until thermometer reads 135 degrees F (57 degrees C) before carving, about 20 minutes. Allrecipes / Christine Ma
