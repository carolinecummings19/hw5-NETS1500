Ingredients:
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 quart stewed tomatoes
- 1 ½ cups uncooked macaroni
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 6 ounces tomato paste (Optional)
- water, as needed

Directions:
- Heat a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
- Reduce heat to low. Add stewed tomatoes, macaroni, onion, garlic, chili powder, and paprika; mix well. Stir in tomato paste. Simmer, adding a little water as needed if the mixture gets too dry, until macaroni is tender, about 20 minutes. Jessica

Nutrition Facts:
565 Calories 25g Fat 57g Carbs 30g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4
