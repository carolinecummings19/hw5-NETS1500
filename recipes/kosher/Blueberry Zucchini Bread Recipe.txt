Ingredients:
- 2 ¼ cups white sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
- 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups shredded zucchini
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 pint fresh blueberries

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease 4 mini loaf pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Beat together sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl. Fold in zucchini. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Beat in flour, cinnamon, salt, baking powder, and baking soda until just combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Gently fold in blueberries. Pour batter into the prepared pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until a knife inserted in the center of a loaf comes out clean, about 50 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cool in the pans for 20 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
