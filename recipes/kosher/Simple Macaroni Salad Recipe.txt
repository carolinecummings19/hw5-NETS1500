Ingredients:
- 1 (16 ounce) package macaroni pasta
- 2 red bell peppers, chopped
- 1 green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped (Optional)
- Â½ cup chopped green onions
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 packet dry vegetable soup mix

Directions:
- Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water until cool.
- Transfer cooked pasta to a large serving bowl. Add bell peppers, celery, green onions, and olive oil; mix until well combined. Stir in mayonnaise and soup mix until incorporated.
- Chill pasta salad in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving.
