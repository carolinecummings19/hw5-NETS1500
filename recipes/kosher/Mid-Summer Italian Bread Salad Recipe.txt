Ingredients:
- 1 clove garlic, peeled
- 1 (1 pound) loaf Italian bread
- 2 cups chopped fresh basil
- 1 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1 cup cucumber - peeled, seeded and chopped
- 1 cup chopped red onion
- ⅛ cup chopped fresh thyme
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup olive oil, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or as needed

Directions:
- Rub peeled garlic clove around a wooden salad bowl.
- Pull apart or chop bread into bite-sized pieces.
- Combine bread, basil, tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, thyme, and minced garlic in the prepared salad bowl. Add olive oil and vinegar; toss until evenly coated.

Nutrition Facts:
468 Calories 18g Fat 66g Carbs 12g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 4
