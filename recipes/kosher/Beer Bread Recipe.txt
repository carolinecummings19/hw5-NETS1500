Ingredients:
- cooking spray
- 3 cups self-rising flour
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
- Mix flour and sugar together in a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour beer into the flour and sugar mixture. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix the batter, first with a wooden spoon and then with your hands, until well combined. Batter will be sticky. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, 50 to 60 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
