Ingredients:
- 4 medium tomatoes, diced
- 1 mango, peeled, seeded, and diced
- 1 avocado, peeled, pitted, and diced
- 1 jalapeño chile pepper, seeded and minced
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Combine tomatoes, mango, avocado, jalapeño, cilantro, and garlic in a medium bowl; stir in red onion, olive oil, lime juice, and salt. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes before serving to blend the flavors.

Nutrition Facts:
158 Calories 12g Fat 14g Carbs 2g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Additional Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 6
