Ingredients:
- 4 cooked chicken breasts, diced
- 1 rib celery, diced
- 4 green onions, chopped
- 1 Golden Delicious apple - peeled, cored and diced
- ⅓ cup golden raisins
- ⅓ cup seedless green grapes, halved
- ½ cup chopped toasted pecans
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon curry powder or more to taste
- ¾ cup light or nonfat mayonnaise

Directions:
- Combine chicken, celery, onion, apple, raisins, grapes, pecans, pepper, curry powder, and mayonnaise in a bowl; mix well to combine. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Nutrition Facts:
229 Calories 14g Fat 12g Carbs 15g Protein
