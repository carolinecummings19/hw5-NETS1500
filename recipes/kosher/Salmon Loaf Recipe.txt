Ingredients:
- 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, undrained
- ½ cup crushed saltine crackers
- ½ cup milk
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Combine salmon, crushed crackers, milk, egg, melted butter, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl. Mix thoroughly.
- Press the salmon mixture into a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
- Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes or until done.

Nutrition Facts:
293 Calories 16g Fat 8g Carbs 28g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 45 mins
Total Time: 50 mins
Servings: 4
Yield: 1 loaf
