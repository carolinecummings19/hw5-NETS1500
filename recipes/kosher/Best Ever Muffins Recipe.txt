Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup vegetable oil

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar together in a large bowl; make a well in the center. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Beat egg with a fork in a small bowl or 2-cup measuring cup; whisk in milk and oil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour egg mixture all at once into flour mixture; mix quickly and lightly with a fork until just moistened. The batter will be lumpy. (Fold in additional ingredients if using; see variations below). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 25 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Flour: This muffin recipe starts with all-purpose flour.
- Baking powder: Baking powder acts as a leavener, which means it helps the muffins rise.
- Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it won’t make the muffins taste salty.
- Sugar: White sugar lends sweetness to the muffins.
- Egg: An egg adds moisture and helps bind the muffin batter together.
- Milk: A cup of milk gives the muffins moisture and flavor.
- Oil: A neutral oil, such as vegetable oil, adds moisture without imparting flavor.
- Stir the dry ingredients together and make a well in the center.
- Beat the egg, then whisk in the milk and oil.
- Pour the egg mixture into the flour mixture and stir.
- Spoon the batter into prepared muffin cups.
- Bake until the tops spring back when lightly pressed.
- Blueberry Muffins: Add 1 cup fresh blueberries.
- Raisin Muffins: Add 1 cup finely chopped raisins.
- Date Muffins: Add 1 cup finely chopped dates.
- Cheese Muffins: Fold in 1 cup grated sharp yellow cheese.
- Bacon Muffins: Fold in 1/4 cup crisp cooked bacon, broken into bits.
