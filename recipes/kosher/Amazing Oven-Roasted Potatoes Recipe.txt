Ingredients:
- 6 medium potatoes, cut into chunks
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Rinse potatoes and dry with a paper towel.
- Place potatoes into a large bowl with oil, seasoned salt, salt, and pepper; mix until well coated. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour onto the prepared baking sheet and spread into an even layer. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven, turning occasionally, until potatoes are tender and golden brown, about 1 hour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
