Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, spinach, water chestnuts, and dry soup mix together in a medium bowl. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Slice off the top of the sourdough round and pull out the soft interior, leaving a sturdy bread bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Fill with spinach mixture. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
