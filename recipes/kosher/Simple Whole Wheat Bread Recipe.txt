Ingredients:
- 3 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- â…” cup honey, divided
- 5 cups bread flour
- 5 tablespoons butter, melted, divided
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 4 cups whole wheat flour, or more as needed

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix warm water, yeast, and 1/3 cup honey in a large bowl to dissolve. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add 5 cups bread flour, and stir to combine. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Let sit for 30 minutes, or until big and bubbly. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix in 3 tablespoons melted butter, remaining 1/3 cup honey, and salt. Stir in 2 cups whole wheat flour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer dough to a floured work surface and gradually knead in remaining 2 cups whole wheat flour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Knead until dough starts to pull away from the work surface, adding more whole wheat flour if necessary; dough should be a bit tacky to the touch, but not too sticky. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place in a greased bowl, turning once to coat the surface of the dough. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover with a dish towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled, 45 minutes to 1 hour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Grease three 9x5-inch loaf pans. Punch down the dough, and divide it into 3 loaves. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place in the prepared loaf pans, and allow to rise until dough has topped the pans by one inch, another 45 minutes to 1 hour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bake the risen loaves in the preheated oven until golden brown for 25 to 30 minutes, do not overbake. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Lightly brush the tops of the loaves with remaining 2 tablespoons melted butter when done to prevent crust from getting hard. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cool completely. Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
