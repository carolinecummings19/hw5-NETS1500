Ingredients:
- 5 roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 10 green onions
- 2 fresh jalapeño peppers, seeded
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Pulse tomatoes, green onions, jalapeño peppers, and cilantro in a blender or food processor to desired consistency. Transfer to a serving bowl. Mix in lime juice, hot pepper sauce, black pepper, garlic powder, and salt until well combined.

Nutrition Facts:
9 Calories 0g Fat 2g Carbs 1g Protein
