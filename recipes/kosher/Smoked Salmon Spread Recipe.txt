Ingredients:
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 12 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill weed
- 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
- 3 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
- Stir cream cheese in a bowl until no longer in block form. Add salmon, green onion, dill, Worcestershire sauce, and hot pepper sauce; mix until well combined.

Nutrition Facts:
164 Calories 14g Fat 1g Carbs 8g Protein
