Ingredients:
- 6 cucumbers, diced
- 4 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
- 5 green onions, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- ⅓ cup chopped garlic
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
- ½ cup minced fresh mint leaves
- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

Directions:
- Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, bell pepper, garlic, parsley, and mint together in a bowl.
- Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over salad. Add salt and pepper, and toss to coat.

Nutrition Facts:
272 Calories 22g Fat 18g Carbs 4g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 25 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 5
