Ingredients:
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ⅓ cup margarine, softened
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 large egg
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C ). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cream together peanut butter and margarine in a medium bowl. Gradually blend in white sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla; mix until fluffy. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Stir into the peanut butter mixture until well blended. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when touched, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool and cut into 16 squares. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
