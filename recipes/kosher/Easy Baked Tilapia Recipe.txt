Ingredients:
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
- 2 teaspoons butter
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®), or to taste
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower with broccoli and red pepper
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees F). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
- Place tilapia fillets in the bottom of the baking dish, then dot with butter and season with garlic salt and seafood seasoning. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios.
- Top each fillet with a slice or two of lemon. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios.
- Arrange frozen mixed vegetables around fillets and season lightly with salt and pepper. Cover the dish with aluminum foil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios.
- Bake in the preheated oven until vegetables are tender and fish flakes easily with a fork, 25 to 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios.
- Serve hot and enjoy! DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS
