Ingredients:
- 3 cups bread flour
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Â¼ cup dry milk powder
- 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 large egg white
- 2 tablespoons water

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place bread flour, sugar, salt, milk powder, 1 cup warm water, butter, and yeast into a bread machine in the order listed or follow the order recommended by the manufacturer if different. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Select Dough cycle; press Start. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Meanwhile, lightly grease a baking sheet.
- Remove dough from the machine, deflate, and turn out onto a lightly floured surface. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Divide dough into 12 equal pieces, form into rounds, and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover with a damp cloth; let rise until doubled in size, about 40 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Mix together egg white and 2 tablespoons water in a small bowl; brush lightly onto the tops of risen rolls. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until rolls are golden brown, about 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
