Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 8 cups shredded red cabbage
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 tart apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 Â½ teaspoons salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
- 3 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, or to taste

Directions:
- Heat oil in a medium saucepan. Add cabbage and onion; cook until wilted. Stir in apples, water, salt, and black pepper; cover and simmer about 25 minutes.
- Stir in sugar and vinegar; taste for desired sweet and sour level. Cook 5 to 6 minutes more.
