Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Melt butter with olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add asparagus spears; cook, stirring occasionally, until desired firmness, about 10 minutes.
- Drain off and discard excess oil; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.

Nutrition Facts:
199 Calories 18g Fat 4g Carbs 8g Protein
