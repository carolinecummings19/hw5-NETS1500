- 2 tablespoons white sugar

- Add oil, flour, and salt to the yeast. Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Jennifer Wendorf / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
- Remove loaf from the machine after the cycle is done; cool in the pan for 5 minutes before turning it out onto a rack to cool completely. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Jennifer Wendorf / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
- Slice, serve, and enjoy! Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Jennifer Wendorf / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
