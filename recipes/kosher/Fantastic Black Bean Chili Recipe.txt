- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
- Heat oil in a large heavy pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add turkey; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
