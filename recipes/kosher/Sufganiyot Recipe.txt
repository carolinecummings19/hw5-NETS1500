Ingredients:
- 4 cups self-rising flour
- 2 (8 ounce) containers yogurt
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- 3 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
- Combine flour, yogurt, eggs, sugar, vanilla sugar, and salt in a large bowl; mix well. Set the dough aside for 30 minutes. Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
- Form dough into 2-inch balls. Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
- Heat vegetable oil to 365 degrees F (190 degrees C) in a large pot or deep fryer over high heat. Deep-fry sufganiyot, in batches, in hot oil until golden brown, about 3 minutes, turning them halfway through. Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
- Use a slotted spoon to transfer donuts to a paper towel-lined plate; repeat with remaining dough. Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
- Enjoy! Allrecipes/Abbey Littlejohn
