Ingredients:
- 2 acorn squash, halved and seeded
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup butter, diced
- 6 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Place squash in a shallow baking pan, cut-side down. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in preheated oven until tender, about 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Turn cut-side up; season with salt and pepper, dot with butter, and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake for 20 minutes more. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

320 Calories 12g Fat 57g Carbs 3g Protein
