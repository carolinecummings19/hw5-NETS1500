Ingredients:
- 4 pounds beef chuck roast
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
- 3 bay leaves
- 6 cups water
- 1 cup soy sauce

Directions:
- Place beef in a slow cooker; season with garlic and rosemary.
- Add bay leaves; pour in water and soy sauce. Cook on Low for 6 to 10 hours.

Nutrition Facts:
290 Calories 20g Fat 3g Carbs 23g Protein
