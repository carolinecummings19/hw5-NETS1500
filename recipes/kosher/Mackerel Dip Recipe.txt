Ingredients:
- 1 (15 ounce) can mackerel, drained and rinsed
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- Â¼ cup tomato-based hot pepper sauce
- 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
- 1 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
- Remove and discard mackerel skin and bones. In a medium bowl, mix mackerel with onion and hot pepper sauce with a fork, breaking it into small pieces. Mix in mayonnaise, then season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.

Servings: 12
