Ingredients:
- 2 pounds red potatoes, cut into quarters
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (250 degrees C) and gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place potatoes in a large roasting pan and toss with oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary until evenly coated. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spread out potatoes in a single layer. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake, stirring occasionally, in the preheated oven until tender, about 20 minutes. Serve warm. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve hot and enjoy! DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS

Nutrition Facts:
