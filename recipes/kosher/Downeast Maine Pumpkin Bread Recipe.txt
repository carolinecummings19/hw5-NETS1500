Ingredients:
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- ⅔ cup water
- 3 cups white sugar
- 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9x5-inch loaf pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Whisk flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger together in a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix pumpkin puree, eggs, oil, water, and sugar in a separate bowl until well blended. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir flour mixture into pumpkin mixture until just blended. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour batter into the prepared pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 50 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS
