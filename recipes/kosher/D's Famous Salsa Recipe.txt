Ingredients:
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
- ½ onion, finely diced
- ¼ cup canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- ½ lime, juiced
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Place tomatoes, onion, green chiles, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, and salt in a blender or food processor. Blend on low to desired consistency.

Nutrition Facts:
16 Calories 0g Fat 4g Carbs 1g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 16
