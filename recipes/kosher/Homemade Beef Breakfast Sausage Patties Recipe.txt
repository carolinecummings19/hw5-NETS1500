Ingredients:
- 2 pounds ground beef (75% to 80% lean)
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons dried sage
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons dried basil
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon dried marjoram
- ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place ground beef into a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir brown sugar, sage, salt, basil, black pepper, onion powder, marjoram, and red pepper flakes together in a small bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Sprinkle over beef and use your hands to mix until evenly distributed. Cover and refrigerate until flavors have blended, about 24 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Divide beef mixture into 8 patties. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Working in batches, cook patties in the hot skillet until firm and no longer pink in the center, 5 to 7 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
