Ingredients:
- 4 cups fresh blueberries
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ cup mayonnaise

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Place blueberries into an 8-inch square baking dish.
- Combine flour, sugar, and cinnamon in a medium bowl. Stir in mayonnaise until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over the top of blueberries.
- Bake in the preheated oven until the top is lightly browned, 35 to 40 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
240 Calories 10g Fat 37g Carbs 2g Protein
