Ingredients:
- 1 (3 pound) boneless chuck roast
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place roast into slow cooker. Sprinkle with garlic powder and onion powder. Season with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour barbeque sauce over meat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove meat from slow cooker, shred, and return to slow cooker. Cook for 1 more hour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve hot. Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

344 Calories 18g Fat 23g Carbs 21g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 7 hrs
Total Time: 7 hrs 10 mins
Servings: 8
