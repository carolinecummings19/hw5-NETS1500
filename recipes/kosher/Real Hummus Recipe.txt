Ingredients:
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 (19 ounce) can chickpeas, half the liquid reserved
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons tahini
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Chop garlic in a blender. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour chickpeas into the blender, reserving about 1 tablespoon for garnish. Add reserved liquid, lemon juice, tahini, and salt to taste; blend until creamy and well mixed. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer mixture to a medium serving bowl. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle pepper over the top. Garnish with reserved chickpeas. DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS

Nutrition Facts:
54 Calories 3g Fat 7g Carbs 2g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 20
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
