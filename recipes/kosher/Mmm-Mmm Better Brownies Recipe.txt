Ingredients:
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Directions:
- Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch square baking pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix sugar, oil, and vanilla together in a medium bowl; beat in eggs until well combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt together in a separate bowl; gradually stir into egg mixture until well blended. Stir in walnuts. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until the brownie begins to pull away from the edges of the pan, about 20 to 25 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Let cool on a wire rack before cutting into 16 squares. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Total Time: 40 mins
Servings: 16
Yield: 16 brownies
