Ingredients:
- 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt and ground black peper to taste

Directions:
- Combine tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a salad bowl. Sprinkle with lemon juice; season with salt and black pepper. Mix until well combined. For best results, chill salad in the refrigerator before serving.

Nutrition Facts:
46 Calories 0g Fat 10g Carbs 2g Protein
