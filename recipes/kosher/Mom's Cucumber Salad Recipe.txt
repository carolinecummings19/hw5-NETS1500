Ingredients:
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 4 teaspoons distilled white vinegar
- ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
- 4 medium cucumbers, peeled and sliced

Directions:
- Stir mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, dill, and seasoned salt together in a large bowl. Stir in cucumber slices, tossing to coat.

Nutrition Facts:
237 Calories 22g Fat 11g Carbs 1g Protein
