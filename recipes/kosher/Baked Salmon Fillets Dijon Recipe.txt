Ingredients:
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
- 3 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup Italian-style dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup butter, melted

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a shallow baking pan with aluminum foil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place salmon fillets skin-side down on the prepared baking pan. Spread a thin layer of mustard on top of each fillet; season with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Top with bread crumbs, then drizzle with melted butter. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Baked Salmon Fillets with Dijon Mustard. Allrecipes
