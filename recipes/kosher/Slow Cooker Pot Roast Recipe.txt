Ingredients:
- 4 pounds chuck roast
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 packet dry onion soup mix
- 1 cup water
- 3 carrots, chopped
- 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Season chuck roast with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat; add roast and sear to brown, about 4 minutes per side. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place roast in the slow cooker and sprinkle soup mix on top. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add water, and scatter carrots, potatoes, onion, and celery on top. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 8 hrs 40 mins
Servings: 8
