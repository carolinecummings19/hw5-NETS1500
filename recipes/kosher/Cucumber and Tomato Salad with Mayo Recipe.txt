Ingredients:
- 4 medium fresh tomatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks
- 1 large cucumber, sliced
- ½ red onion, diced
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons fresh ground black pepper
- salt to taste

Directions:
- Toss together tomatoes, cucumber, onion, mayonnaise, and garlic in a large serving bowl. Season with pepper and salt.

Nutrition Facts:
142 Calories 11g Fat 10g Carbs 2g Protein
