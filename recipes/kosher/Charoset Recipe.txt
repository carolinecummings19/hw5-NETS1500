Ingredients:
- 2 Granny Smith apples, peeled and coarsely grated
- 1 cup walnuts, finely ground
- 2 tablespoons grape juice
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
- Mix apples, walnuts, grape juice, honey, and cinnamon together in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, 8 hours to overnight. Allrecipes

Nutrition Facts:
98 Calories 8g Fat 7g Carbs 2g Protein
