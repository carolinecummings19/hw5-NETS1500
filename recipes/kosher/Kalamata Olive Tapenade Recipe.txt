Ingredients:
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 cup pitted kalamata olives
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place garlic cloves into a blender or food processor; pulse to mince. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add olives, parsley, capers, lemon juice, and olive oil. Blend until mixture is finely chopped. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Season with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Prep Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 1 cup
