Ingredients:
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup milk
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 pinch salt
- 12 thick slices bread

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. ALLRECIPES / DIANA CHISTRUGA
- Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth. ALLRECIPES / DIANA CHISTRUGA
- Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated. ALLRECIPES / DIANA CHISTRUGA
- Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. ALLRECIPES / DIANA CHISTRUGA
- Serve hot and enjoy. ALLRECIPES / DIANA CHISTRUGA

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 30 mins
Servings: 12
