Ingredients:
- ½ cup red wine
- ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ cup liquid smoke flavoring
- 4 teaspoons celery flakes
- 4 teaspoons pepper
- 2 teaspoons onion salt
- 2 teaspoons garlic salt

Directions:
- Combine wine, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke in a medium bowl. Stir in celery flakes, pepper, onion salt, and garlic salt.

Nutrition Facts:
115 Calories 9g Fat 5g Carbs 0g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 5 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 1 cup
