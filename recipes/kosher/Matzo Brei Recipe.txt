Ingredients:
- 1 matzo cracker
- 1 large egg
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon butter or vegetable oil

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Jason Donnelly / Food Stylist: Holly Dressman / Prop Stylist: Natalie Ghazali
- Break matzo into pieces in a bowl; cover with hot water and soak until matzo just begins to soften, about 30 to 60 seconds. Drain and squeeze out water; set matzo aside. Jason Donnelly / Food Stylist: Holly Dressman / Prop Stylist: Natalie Ghazali
- Beat egg, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl until fluffy; mix in matzo. Jason Donnelly / Food Stylist: Holly Dressman / Prop Stylist: Natalie Ghazali
- Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat; pour matzo-egg mixture into skillet. Cook until egg is cooked on one side, about 1 minute; flip and cook on other side, about 1 minute more. Jason Donnelly / Food Stylist: Holly Dressman / Prop Stylist: Natalie Ghazali
