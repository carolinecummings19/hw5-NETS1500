Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
- 4 large eggs
- 3 cups grated zucchini
- ¾ cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt together in a medium bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add oil and eggs; mix well until just combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Fold in zucchini and walnuts until they are evenly distributed. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour cake batter into the prepared pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 50 to 60 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cool cake completely before frosting with your favorite frosting. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
