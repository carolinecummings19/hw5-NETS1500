Ingredients:
- 1 pound salmon fillets
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 5 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- sea salt to taste
- freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish. AllRecipes / Ana Cadena
- Place salmon in the baking dish. AllRecipes / Ana Cadena
- Mix butter and lemon juice in a small bowl, and drizzle over salmon. Season with dill, garlic powder, sea salt, and pepper. AllRecipes / Ana Cadena
- Bake in the preheated oven until salmon is easily flaked with a fork, about 25 minutes. AllRecipes / Ana Cadena
- Serve and enjoy! AllRecipes / Ana Cadena
