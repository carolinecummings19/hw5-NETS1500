Ingredients:
- 1 pound bone-in chicken parts
- 1 large onion, quartered
- 3 stalks celery ribs with leaves, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 large carrot, cut into 1-inch chunks
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 3 whole cloves
- 6 cups water
- ¼ cup cold water (Optional)
- 1 large egg (Optional)

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Place chicken parts, onion, celery, carrot, salt, and cloves in a large soup pot or Dutch oven. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Add 6 cups water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 1 hour. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Remove chicken and vegetables. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Strain stock. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Skim fat off the surface. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- To clarify stock for clear soup, removing solid flecks that are too small to be strained out with cheesecloth, follow this method: Separate egg white from egg yolk and reserve the shell. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Combine 1/4 cup cold water, egg white, and crushed eggshell in a small bowl. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Add to strained stock and bring to a boil. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Strain again through a sieve lined with cheesecloth. Rachel Marek / Food Stylist: Addelyn Evans / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
