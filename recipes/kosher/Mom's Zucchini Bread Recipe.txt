Ingredients:
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 3 large eggs
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 Â¼ cups white sugar
- 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8x4-inch pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Sift flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Beat eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla together in a separate large bowl with an electric mixer until combined; add flour mixture and beat well. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in zucchini and walnuts until well combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour batter into the prepared pans. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 40 to 60 minutes. Cool in the pans on a wire rack for 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Run a table knife around the edges to loosen. Invert carefully onto a wire rack and let cool completely. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
