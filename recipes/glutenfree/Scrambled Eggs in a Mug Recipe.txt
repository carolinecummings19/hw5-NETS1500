Ingredients:
- 1 large egg
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 pinch ground black pepper (Optional)

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Crack egg into a microwave-safe mug; beat until even in color. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour in milk and beat until light yellow in color. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Microwave on high until cooked through and fluffy, about 90 seconds. Season with pepper and serve. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
