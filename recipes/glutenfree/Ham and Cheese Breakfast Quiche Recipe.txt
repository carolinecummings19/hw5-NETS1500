Ingredients:
- 2 (12 ounce) packages frozen hash brown potatoes
- ⅓ cup butter, melted
- 1 cup cooked diced ham
- 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
- Squeeze hash browns to remove excess moisture, then combine with melted butter in a small bowl. Press mixture into the bottom and up the sides of an ungreased 10-inch pie pan.
- Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
- Remove from the oven and arrange ham and cheese evenly over crust. In a small bowl, beat together eggs and cream; pour over ham and cheese.
- Return to the oven and bake for 30 minutes, or until custard has completely set. Unknown

Nutrition Facts:
461 Calories 33g Fat 25g Carbs 18g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 55 mins
Total Time: 1 hr 15 mins
Servings: 5
Yield: 1 10-inch quiche
