Ingredients:
- 1 (8 ounce) package pitted dates
- 4 ounces almonds
- 1 pound sliced bacon

Directions:
- Preheat the broiler.
- Slit dates. Place one almond inside each date. Wrap dates with bacon, using toothpicks to hold them together.
- Broil 10 minutes, or until bacon is evenly brown and crisp.

Nutrition Facts:
560 Calories 44g Fat 32g Carbs 14g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 35 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 6 servings
