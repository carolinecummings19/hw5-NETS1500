Ingredients:
- 4 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
- ¾ cup water
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine apples, water, sugar, and cinnamon in a saucepan; cover and cook over medium heat until apples are soft, about 15 to 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Allow apple mixture to cool, then mash with a fork or potato masher until it is the consistency you like. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
