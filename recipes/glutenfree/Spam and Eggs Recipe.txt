Ingredients:
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 1 (12 ounce) container fully cooked luncheon meat (e.g. Spam), cubed
- 2 ounces Cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
- Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Pour eggs into the skillet, then scatter luncheon meat over top. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3 to 4 minutes. Sprinkle cheese over top and stir until cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes.
