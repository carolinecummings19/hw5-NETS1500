Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons ground pork
- 1 ½ tablespoons chopped garlic
- 4 cups cooked white rice
- 1 ½ teaspoons garlic salt
- ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground pork and garlic in hot oil until garlic is golden brown; do not allow garlic to burn, or it will taste bitter. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in cooked rice; season with garlic salt and black pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cook and stir until heated through and well blended, about 3 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
