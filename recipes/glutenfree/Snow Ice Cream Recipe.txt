Ingredients:
- 1 gallon clean snow
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups milk

Directions:
- When it starts to snow, place a large, clean bowl outside to collect flakes.
- When the bowl is full, mix in sugar and vanilla. Stir in just enough milk for desired consistency. Serve at once. Melissa Goff
