Ingredients:
- 1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup minced celery
- Â½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 1 teaspoon minced onion
- 1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
- Mix drained pineapple, cream cheese, celery, bell pepper, and onion together in a medium bowl until well combined; divide mixture in half and form two balls.
- Place chopped pecans in a shallow dish; roll each ball in pecans. Chill until ready to serve, at least 2 hours.
