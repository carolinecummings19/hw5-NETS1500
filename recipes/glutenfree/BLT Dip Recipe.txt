Ingredients:
- 1 pound bacon
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tomato - peeled, seeded and diced

Directions:
- Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.
- Combine mayonnaise and sour cream in a medium bowl. Crumble bacon into mayonnaise mixture; mix until combined. Stir in tomatoes right before serving.
