Ingredients:
- 2 ½ cups gluten-free flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup white sugar
- ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- ¼ cup cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Make the cookies: Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Beat white sugar, butter, and vanilla in a bowl with an electric mixer until creamy, 3 to 4 minutes. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, until mixture is smooth. Gradually mix in flour mixture until a dough forms. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Form dough into a ball. Wrap ball with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or silicon baking mats. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Roll dough on a lightly floured surface and cut into shapes using desired cookie cutters. Arrange cookies on the prepared baking sheets. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Bake in the preheated oven until edges begin to brown, about 10 minutes, switching racks halfway through. Cool on the baking sheet briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool, 8 to 10 minutes more. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- While the cookies are cooling, make the icing: Beat cream cheese, butter, and vanilla in a bowl using an electric mixer until well combined. Slowly beat in confectioners' sugar until icing is smooth. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
- Spread icing onto cooled cookies. Allrecipes/Diana Chistruga
