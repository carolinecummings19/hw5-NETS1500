Ingredients:
- 1 cup almond flour
- ¼ cup water
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon oil, or as needed

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Whisk almond flour, water, eggs, maple syrup, and salt together in a large bowl until smooth. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat oil on a griddle over medium heat. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, 3 to 5 minutes. Flip and cook until bottoms are browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Repeat with remaining batter. Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Prep Time: 10 mins
