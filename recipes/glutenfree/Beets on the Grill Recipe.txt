Ingredients:
- cooking spray
- 6 beets, scrubbed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Coat one side of a large piece of aluminum foil with cooking spray. Place beets and butter on foil; season with salt and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Wrap foil over beets. Place packet on the grill grate. Cook until beets are very tender, about 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Allow beets to cool before serving, about 5 minutes. You don't even need to peel to enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
