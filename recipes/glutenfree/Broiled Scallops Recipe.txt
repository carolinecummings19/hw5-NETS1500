Ingredients:
- 1 Â½ pounds bay scallops
- 2 tablespoons melted butter, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon garlic salt

Directions:
- Preheat the broiler to high heat.
- Rinse scallops and place in a shallow baking pan. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons melted butter, lemon juice, and garlic salt.
- Broil in the preheated broiler until scallops begin to turn golden, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove from the oven and serve with extra melted butter on the side for dipping. Spunky Buddy
