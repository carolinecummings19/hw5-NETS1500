Ingredients:
- 1 (1 pound) loaf gluten-free bread
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 medium onions, diced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh sage
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
- Â¾ teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups gluten-free chicken broth
- 2 large eggs, beaten

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 3-quart baking dish. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cut bread into 3/4-inch cubes and spread into a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, 12 to 17 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and celery; cook and stir until soft, 8 to 10 minutes. Stir in sage, thyme, salt, and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add toasted bread cubes, chicken broth, and beaten eggs to the skillet. Stir until well combined, then transfer mixture to a greased 3-quart baking dish. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover and continue baking until the top is crisp and lightly browned, about 10 more minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
