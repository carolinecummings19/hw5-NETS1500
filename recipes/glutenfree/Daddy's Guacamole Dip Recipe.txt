Ingredients:
- 2 avocados, halved with pits removed
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 2 cups cottage cheese
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- ½ cup diced sweet onion
- 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Scoop avocado flesh out of the skins and place into a medium mixing bowl; discard skins. Add lemon juice to the bowl and mash well. Add cottage cheese, tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, garlic powder, salt, and pepper; stir until combined. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.

Nutrition Facts:
51 Calories 3g Fat 3g Carbs 3g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 25 mins
Additional Time: 1 hr
Total Time: 1 hr 25 mins
Servings: 24
Yield: 3 cups
