Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 ½ pounds fresh mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Directions:
- Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms; season with Italian seasoning and cook 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
- Stir wine and garlic into the skillet; cook until most of the wine has evaporated. Season with salt and black pepper; sprinkle with chives. Cook 1 minute.

Nutrition Facts:
57 Calories 3g Fat 6g Carbs 2g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 6
