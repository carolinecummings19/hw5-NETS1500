Ingredients:
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 large egg
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
- Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) according to manufacturer's instructions. Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper.
- Combine mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and garlic powder in a bowl until well mixed. Press into a circle on the parchment in the basket.
- Air-fry for 10 minutes. Serve warm, but not hot.

Nutrition Facts:
225 Calories 14g Fat 3g Carbs 21g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 2
