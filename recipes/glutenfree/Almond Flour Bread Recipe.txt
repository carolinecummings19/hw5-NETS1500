Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups almond flour
- ⅓ cup flaxseed meal
- 4 large eggs
- ¼ cup unsweetened almond milk
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan, or line with parchment paper.
- Mix almond flour, flaxseed meal, eggs, almond milk, olive oil, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl until a soft dough forms. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer to the prepared loaf pan. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 40 minutes. Cool in the loaf pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

229 Calories 20g Fat 7g Carbs 9g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Additional Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 1 hr
Servings: 8
Yield: 1 (8 x 4-inch) loaf
