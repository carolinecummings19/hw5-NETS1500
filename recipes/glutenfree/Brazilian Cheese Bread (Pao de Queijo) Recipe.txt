Ingredients:
- ½ cup olive oil or butter
- ⅓ cup water
- ⅓ cup milk or soy milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups tapioca flour
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- ⅔ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 beaten eggs

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Combine olive oil, water, milk, and salt in a large saucepan and place over high heat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bring to a boil and immediately remove from the heat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in tapioca flour and garlic, stirring until smooth. Set aside to rest for 10 to 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir cheese and eggs into tapioca mixture until combined; the dough will be chunky, like cottage cheese. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Drop dough by 1/4 cup-size balls onto an ungreased baking sheet. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
