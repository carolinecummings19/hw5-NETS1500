Ingredients:
- 2 teaspoons coconut oil
- 3 ½ cups almond flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 large ripe bananas
- ¼ cup coconut oil, melted and cooled
- 2 large eggs

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a rimmed baking sheet with 2 teaspoons coconut oil.
- Whisk almond flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Break bananas into pieces and place into a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave until soft, about 30 seconds. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mash bananas until creamy. Add 1/4 cup melted coconut oil and eggs; mix until well combined. Stir in dry ingredients until incorporated. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Drop batter onto the prepared baking sheet, making 12 biscuit-shaped mounds. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until peaks are lightly browned, about 17 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly before serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
