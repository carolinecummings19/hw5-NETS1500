Ingredients:
- 12 large eggs

Directions:
- Place eggs in a pot; pour enough water over the eggs to cover. Cover the pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
- Remove pot from the heat; let sit, covered, for 15 minutes.
- Fill a large bowl halfway with cold water; transfer eggs into cold water. Replace the water with cold water as needed to keep cold until eggs are completely cooled. Chill eggs in refrigerator for at least 2 hours before peeling.
