Ingredients:
- 1 pound chicken livers
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- ¾ cup butter, softened
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 3 tablespoons cooking sherry
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground mace

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine livers and sliced onion in a medium saucepan. Pour in about 3 cups water and bring to a boil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until liver is tender, about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove from heat and drain. Discard sliced onion, and remove and discard any hard portions of the livers. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Process cooked livers in a food processor until smooth. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add butter, chopped onion, sherry, butter, salt, pepper, and mace; pulse to blend. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Form mixture into a mound with lightly greased hands. Place onto a serving platter; cover and chill for 1 hour before serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
