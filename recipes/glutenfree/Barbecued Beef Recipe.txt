Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups ketchup
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 (4 pound) boneless chuck roast

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine ketchup, brown sugar, red wine vinegar, Dijon-style mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place chuck roast in a slow cooker. Pour ketchup mixture over chuck roast. Cover and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove chuck roast from slow cooker, shred with a fork, and return to the slow cooker. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir meat to evenly coat with sauce. Continue cooking for approximately 1 hour more. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
