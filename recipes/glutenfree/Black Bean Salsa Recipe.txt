Ingredients:
- 3 (15 ounce) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn, drained
- 2 (10 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, partially drained
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 2 bunches green onions, chopped
- cilantro leaves, for garnish

Directions:
- Mix black beans, Mexican-style corn, diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, tomatoes, and green onion stalks together in a large bowl. Garnish with desired amount of cilantro leaves.
