Ingredients:
- 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons light brown sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ½ teaspoon ground mustard
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon oregano
- 6 bone-in center-cut pork chops (about 1-inch thick)

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients and preheat the smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Add wood chips according to the manufacturer's directions. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine salt, brown sugar, pepper, paprika, ground mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, and oregano in a small bowl. Sprinkle over both sides pork chops. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place pork chops, unstacked, onto smoker grate. Smoke for 2 hours 30 minutes. Remove chops from the smoker and let rest for 10 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Nutrition Facts:
193 Calories 8g Fat 2g Carbs 26g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins
Additional Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 2 hrs 50 mins
Servings: 6
