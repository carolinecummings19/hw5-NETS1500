Ingredients:
- ½ cup unsalted butter
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- ½ (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 ½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 pinch salt, or to taste
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or to taste
- 1 pinch ground white pepper, or to taste

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in heavy cream and cream cheese. Gradually add Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until well incorporated and melted, about 5 to 7 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in salt, nutmeg, and white pepper. The sauce will thicken as it cools. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
