Ingredients:
- 1 (16 ounce) package bacon

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place bacon slices, one next to the other, on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove from the oven to flip bacon slices with kitchen tongs and then return to the oven. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake until crispy, 15 to 20 minutes more. Thinner slices will need less time, about 20 minutes total. Drain on a plate lined with paper towels. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! Allrecipes / Qi Ai
