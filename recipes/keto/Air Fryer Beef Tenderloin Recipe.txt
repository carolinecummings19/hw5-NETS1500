Ingredients:
- 2 pounds beef tenderloin, at room temperature
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Â½ teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pat dry tenderloin with paper towels and place on a plate. Drizzle oil all over meat, then sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Rub spices and oil into meat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place into the preheated air fryer basket, folding as needed to make it fit. Close the lid. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Reduce heat to 390 degrees F (198 degrees C) and cook for 22 minutes. Reduce heat to 360 degrees F (182 degrees C) and cook for 10 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of tenderloin should read 135 degrees F (57 degrees C) for medium doneness. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove tenderloin to a platter. Allow to rest, uncovered, for at least 10 minutes before serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
