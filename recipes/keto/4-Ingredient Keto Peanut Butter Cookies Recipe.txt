Ingredients:
- 1 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup low-calorie natural sweetener (such as Swerve®)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon sugar-free vanilla extract

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine peanut butter, sweetener, egg, and vanilla extract in a bowl; mix well until a dough is formed. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Roll dough into 12 (1-inch) balls. Place on the prepared baking sheet and press down twice with a fork in a criss-cross pattern. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 12 to 15 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cool for 1 minute before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Nutrition Facts:
133 Calories 11g Fat 12g Carbs 6g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 12
Yield: 12 cookies
