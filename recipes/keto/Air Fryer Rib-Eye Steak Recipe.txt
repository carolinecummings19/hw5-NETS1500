Ingredients:
- 2 rib-eye steaks, cut 1 1/2- inch thick
- ½ cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 4 teaspoons grill seasoning (such as Montreal Steak Seasoning®)

Directions:
- Combine steaks, soy sauce, olive oil, and seasoning in a large resealable bag. Marinate meat for at least 2 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove steaks from the bag and discard marinade. Pat excess oil off steaks. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add about 1 tablespoon water to the bottom of the air fryer pan to prevent it from smoking during cooking. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cook steaks in the preheated air fryer for 7 minutes, flip, and cook about 7 minutes more for medium-rare. For a medium, cook 8 minutes per side. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Let steaks sit for about 5 minutes before serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
