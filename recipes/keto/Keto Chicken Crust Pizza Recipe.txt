Ingredients:
- 1 pound ground chicken
- ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix ground chicken, mozzarella, Parmesan, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl with your hands until well combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Fold in basil. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place chicken mixture between two pieces of parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Gently press on the top piece of parchment and spread mixture into a circle or rectangle with a thickness of 1/4 to 1/2 inch. Remove and discard the top piece of parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until starting to brown on the edges, 35 to 45 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
