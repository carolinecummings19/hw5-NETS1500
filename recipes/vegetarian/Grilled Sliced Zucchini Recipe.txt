Ingredients:
- 1 large zucchini
- Â¼ cup Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
- Preheat an outdoor grill and lightly oil the grate.
- Cut zucchini into 1/4-inch slices. Toss in a large bowl with Italian dressing until evenly coated.
- Cook on the preheated grill until grill marks appear and zucchini is slightly limp, 4 to 5 minutes.
