Ingredients:
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 (16 ounce) cans refried beans
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- ½ (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 5 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Mix cream cheese and sour cream together in a medium bowl until well blended. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix in refried beans, 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, 1/2 of the Monterey Jack cheese, green onions, parsley, taco seasoning, and hot pepper sauce. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer mixture to an 8x12-inch baking dish and sprinkle remaining Cheddar and Monterey Jack over top. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until dip is hot and bubbly and cheese is golden brown, 20 to 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
