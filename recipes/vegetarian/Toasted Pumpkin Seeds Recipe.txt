Ingredients:
- 2 cups raw whole pumpkin seeds
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
- Spread the pumpkin seeds on a medium baking sheet. Drizzle with oil. Sprinkle with salt.
- Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, stirring occasionally, until lightly toasted.

Nutrition Facts:
102 Calories 7g Fat 9g Carbs 3g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 45 mins
Total Time: 50 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 2 cups
