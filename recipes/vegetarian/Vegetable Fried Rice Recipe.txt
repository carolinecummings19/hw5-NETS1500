Ingredients:
- 3 cups water
- 1 ½ cups quick-cooking brown rice
- 2 tablespoons peanut oil
- 1 small yellow onion, chopped
- 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 3 green onions, thinly sliced
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 cup frozen petite peas
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil
- ¼ cup roasted peanuts (Optional)

Directions:
- Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in rice. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Set aside. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat peanut oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add onions, bell pepper, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Increase heat to medium-high. Stir in cooked rice, green onions, and soy sauce; cook and stir for 1 minute. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add peas and cook for 1 minute more. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Remove from heat. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with peanuts. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Total Time: 55 mins
Servings: 4
