Ingredients:
- 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked rotini pasta
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing
- 2 medium cucumbers, chopped
- 6 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 4 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Place pasta in the pot, cook for 8 to 12 minutes, until al dente, and drain.
- Toss cooked pasta with Italian dressing, cucumbers, tomatoes, and green onions in a large bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix Parmesan cheese and Italian seasoning in a small bowl, and gently mix into the salad. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Refrigerate pasta salad until chilled for best results, at least 30 minutes before serving. Enjoy! DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS
