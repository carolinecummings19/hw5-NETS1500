Ingredients:
- 1 (15 ounce) package refrigerated pie crusts
- 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (12 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 1 cup finely chopped onion
- 12 large eggs
- 2 cups milk
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Morgan Hunt Glaze / Food Stylist: Torie Cox / Prop Stylist: Abby Armstrong
- Line two 9-inch pie plates with crusts. Cover crusts with a double layer of aluminum foil and a layer of pie weights or dried beans. Morgan Hunt Glaze / Food Stylist: Torie Cox / Prop Stylist: Abby Armstrong
- Bake in the preheated oven until edge of crust is golden, about 10 minutes. Carefully remove the foil and weights; continue baking until the crust sets, about 5 minutes more. Let crusts cool briefly. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Morgan Hunt Glaze / Food Stylist: Torie Cox / Prop Stylist: Abby Armstrong
- Sprinkle cheese, spinach, and onion evenly onto the crusts. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together and divide evenly between the crusts. Morgan Hunt Glaze / Food Stylist: Torie Cox / Prop Stylist: Abby Armstrong
- Bake in the center of the preheated oven until golden brown and firm in the centers, about 45 minutes. Morgan Hunt Glaze / Food Stylist: Torie Cox / Prop Stylist: Abby Armstrong
