Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- ¾ cup heavy whipping cream
- 3 eggs
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish or pie pan.
- Heat butter and olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; add onion. Cook and stir until softened, about 10 minutes; transfer onion to a plate to cool.
- Whisk cream, eggs, salt, and black pepper in a bowl; stir in mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and onion. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish.
- Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are set and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes. It should be light brown around the edges but not brown on top.
