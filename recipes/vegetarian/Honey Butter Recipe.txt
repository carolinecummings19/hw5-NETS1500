Ingredients:
- ¾ cup butter, room temperature
- ¼ cup honey

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients.
- Mix butter and honey together in a bowl until smooth.
- Cover and store in the refrigerator.

Nutrition Facts:
185 Calories 17g Fat 9g Carbs 0g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 8
