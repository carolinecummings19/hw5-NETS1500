Ingredients:
- 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 ripe tomato, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 lime, juiced
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Season with lime juice and salt and pepper to taste. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
