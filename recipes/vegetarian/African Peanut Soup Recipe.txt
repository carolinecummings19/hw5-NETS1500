Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 2 large red bell peppers, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 cups vegetable broth or stock
- 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes, with liquid
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder (Optional)
- ⅔ cup extra crunchy peanut butter
- ½ cup uncooked brown rice

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Jake Sternquist / Food Stylist: Lauren McAnelly / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Heat oil in a large stock pot over medium-high heat. Add onions and bell peppers; cook and stir until tender. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Jake Sternquist / Food Stylist: Lauren McAnelly / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Stir in vegetable stock, tomatoes, pepper, and chili powder. Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Jake Sternquist / Food Stylist: Lauren McAnelly / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
- Stir in rice; cover and simmer until rice is tender, about 15 minutes. Stir in peanut butter until well blended; serve. Jake Sternquist / Food Stylist: Lauren McAnelly / Prop Stylist: Gabriel Greco
