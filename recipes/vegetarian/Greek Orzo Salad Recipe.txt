Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups uncooked orzo pasta
- 2 (6 ounce) cans marinated artichoke hearts
- 1 cucumber, seeded and chopped
- 1 ripe tomato, seeded and chopped
- 1 red onion, chopped
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1 (2 ounce) can black olives, drained
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon lemon pepper

Directions:
- Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Drain artichoke hearts, reserving liquid. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Gather prepared ingredients together. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine pasta, artichoke hearts, cucumber, tomato, onion, feta, olives, parsley, lemon juice, oregano, and lemon pepper in a large bowl; toss well. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Just before serving, drizzle reserved artichoke marinade over salad. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS
