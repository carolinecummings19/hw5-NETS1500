Ingredients:
- 2 (10 ounce) bags baby spinach
- 2 bananas, sliced
- 12 fresh strawberries, sliced
- 1 (10 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- ½ cup pine nuts
- 1 cup poppy seed salad dressing

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Greg DuPree/ Food Stylist: Ana Kelly / Prop Stylist: Prissy Montiel
- Place spinach in a large bowl. Arrange bananas, strawberries, mandarin oranges, cranberries, and pine nuts on top. Greg DuPree/ Food Stylist: Ana Kelly / Prop Stylist: Prissy Montiel
- Toss with poppy seed dressing before serving. Greg DuPree/ Food Stylist: Ana Kelly / Prop Stylist: Prissy Montiel

Nutrition Facts:
292 Calories 18g Fat 30g Carbs 5g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 8
