Ingredients:
- 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
- 1 cup fresh blueberries
- 1 cup red seedless grapes
- 1 Fuji apple, cored and chopped
- 1 Granny Smith apple, chopped
- 2 kiwi fruit, peeled and chopped
- 1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- 2 cups lemon yogurt

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Morgan Glaze / Food Stylist: Emily Nabors Hall / Prop Stylist: Josh Hoggle
- Combine strawberries, blueberries, grapes, apples, and kiwi in a salad bowl. Morgan Glaze / Food Stylist: Emily Nabors Hall / Prop Stylist: Josh Hoggle
- Add drained pineapple, then yogurt. Mix until thoroughly combined. Morgan Glaze / Food Stylist: Emily Nabors Hall / Prop Stylist: Josh Hoggle
