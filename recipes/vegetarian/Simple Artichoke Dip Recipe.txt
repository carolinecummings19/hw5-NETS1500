Ingredients:
- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix artichoke hearts, mayonnaise, and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl until well combined. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spread mixture in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
