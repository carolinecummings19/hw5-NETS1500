Ingredients:
- aluminum foil
- cooking spray
- 6 (29 ounce) cans yams in syrup, drained
- 1 ½ cups brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Coat two pieces aluminum foil with cooking spray.
- Mash yams in a large bowl with a fork. Add brown sugar, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and heavy cream in the exact order, mixing well after each addition; divide between two 8-inch square baking dishes. Divide marshmallows between each dish to cover; cover with aluminum foil, coated-sides down.
- Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove foil; bake 5 minutes more.

Nutrition Facts:
325 Calories 2g Fat 75g Carbs 3g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Total Time: 35 mins
Servings: 20
Yield: 2 (8-inch) square pans
