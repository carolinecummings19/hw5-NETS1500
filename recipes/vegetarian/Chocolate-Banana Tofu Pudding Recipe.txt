Ingredients:
- 1 banana, broken into chunks
- 1 (12 ounce) package soft silken tofu
- Â¼ cup confectioners' sugar
- 5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 tablespoons soy milk
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
- Place banana, tofu, sugar, cocoa powder, soy milk, and cinnamon into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth.
- Pour into individual serving dishes, and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

124 Calories 4g Fat 21g Carbs 6g Protein
