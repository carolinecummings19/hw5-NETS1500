Ingredients:
- 2 pounds small red potatoes, quartered
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup milk
- ¼ cup sour cream
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add red potatoes, and cook until tender but still firm, about 10 minutes; drain. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer potatoes to a large bowl; add butter, milk, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mash mixture together until smooth and creamy. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
