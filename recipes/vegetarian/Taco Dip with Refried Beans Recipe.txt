Ingredients:
- 3 (16 ounce) cans vegetarian refried beans
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- ½ cup salsa
- ½ head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
- 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- ¾ cup chopped black olives
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
- Spread refried beans over the bottom of a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.
- Mix sour cream and taco seasoning together until combined; spread mixture over beans. Top with a layer of salsa, followed by lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and olives. Sprinkle Cheddar over top.

Nutrition Facts:
172 Calories 10g Fat 14g Carbs 7g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 20
Yield: 1 9x13-inch pan
