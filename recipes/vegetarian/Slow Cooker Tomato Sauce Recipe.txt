Ingredients:
- 10 Roma (plum) tomatoes - peeled, seeded and crushed
- ½ small onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch cinnamon

Directions:
- Place tomatoes, onion, garlic, and olive oil in a slow cooker. Season with oregano, basil, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper, and cinnamon.
- Cover and cook on Low until desired consistency and flavor is reached, about 10 to 15 hours.

Nutrition Facts:
105 Calories 9g Fat 6g Carbs 1g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 10 hrs
Total Time: 10 hrs 15 mins
Servings: 6
