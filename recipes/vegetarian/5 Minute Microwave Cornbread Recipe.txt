Ingredients:
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup cornmeal
- ½ cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Mix together flour, cornmeal, milk, egg, sugar, oil, baking powder, and salt in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl until well combined.
- Heat in the microwave on high until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 3 minutes. Rotate the bowl halfway through cooking if the microwave does not have a turntable.

Nutrition Facts:
154 Calories 6g Fat 21g Carbs 4g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 6
