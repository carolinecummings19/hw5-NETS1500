Ingredients:
- 2 heads cauliflower, broken into florets
- olive oil cooking spray
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 6 cups water
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
- Soak cauliflower florets in a large bowl of lightly salted water for 20 minutes. Drain well, then pat dry. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler. Line a baking sheet with heavy-duty aluminum foil. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
- Arrange cauliflower in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet; spray evenly with cooking spray. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
- Broil in the preheated oven until browned, 20 to 30 minutes. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
- Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in roasted cauliflower and garlic. Pour in water, season with salt and pepper, and simmer until all vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
- Purée soup with an immersion blender until smooth. Jacob Fox / Food Styling: Sammy Mila / Prop Styling: Gabriel Greco
