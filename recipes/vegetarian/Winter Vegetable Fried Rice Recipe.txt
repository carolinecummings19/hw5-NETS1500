Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced or shaved
- 2 cups chopped green cabbage
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1 cup snow peas
- 1/2 cup chopped white onion
- 1/2 cup bean sprouts
- 2 cups cooked brown rice
- 1/3 cup chopped green onion
- 3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons hot chili sauce, or more to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Spray a wok or deep skillet with cooking spray and preheat over medium-high heat. Allrecipes / Julia Hartbeck
- Whisk eggs and water in a bowl until well mixed. Scramble eggs in wok until no longer runny, but still moist, about 1 minute. Remove from wok or skillet; set aside. Allrecipes / Julia Hartbeck
- Wipe out the wok and return to medium-high heat. Add oil and garlic. Cook garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds, stirring frequently. Add cabbage, carrots, snow peas, onions, and sprouts . Cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently to mix ingredients. Allrecipes / Julia Hartbeck
- Add rice, egg, green onion, soy sauce, and chili sauce. and cook until heated through, 3 to 4 minutes, stirring frequently. Allrecipes / Julia Hartbeck
