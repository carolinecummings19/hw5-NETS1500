Ingredients:
- 8 cups fresh broccoli
- ½ cup unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 cups shredded Swiss cheese
- 2 large eggs, beaten

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
- Place broccoli in a steamer over 1 inch boiling water. Cover and cook until crisp-tender, 2 to 6 minutes. Drain.
- Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and stir, cooking until bubbly. Stir in onion, then milk. Bring to a boil and cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper.
- Add cheese and eggs and stir until cheese melts. Add broccoli and stir to coat. Transfer mixture to a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
- Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
441 Calories 33g Fat 15g Carbs 23g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 7
Yield: 1 9x13-inch casserole
