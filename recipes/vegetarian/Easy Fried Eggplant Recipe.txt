Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 large eggplant, peeled and sliced
- 3 large eggs, beaten
- 2 cups dry bread crumbs

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Dip eggplant slices in egg, then coat with bread crumbs. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place breaded eggplant in the hot oil and fry until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Drain on paper towels. Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
