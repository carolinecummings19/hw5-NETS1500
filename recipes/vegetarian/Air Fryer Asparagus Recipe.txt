Ingredients:
- 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
- avocado oil cooking spray
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon Himalayan pink salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground multi-colored peppercorns
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line the basket with parchment paper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place asparagus spears in the prepared basket and mist with avocado oil. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, pepper, and pepper flakes. Top with Parmesan cheese. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Air-fry until asparagus spears start to char, 7 to 9 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
