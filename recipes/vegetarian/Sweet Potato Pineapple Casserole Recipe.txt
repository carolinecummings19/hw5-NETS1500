Ingredients:
- 3 sweet potatoes
- ½ cup crushed pineapple with juice
- ¼ cup packed light brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
- Place whole sweet potatoes in a large pot and cover with water; boil until soft. Transfer to a cutting board; when cool enough to handle, remove skins. Cut into bite-sized pieces; transfer to prepared baking dish.
- Add crushed pineapple, brown sugar, and butter to baking dish; stir to combine.
- Bake in the preheated oven until casserole mushy with no excess water in dish, about 45 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
103 Calories 4g Fat 17g Carbs 1g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Servings: 9
