Ingredients:
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 large onion, diced
- ½ large green bell pepper, diced
- 1 (4 ounce) jar chopped pimento peppers
- 2 large eggs
- 1 (8 ounce) package Jiffy corn muffin mix
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, with liquid
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can creamed corn
- 1 pint sour cream
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, and pimento peppers and saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Combine eggs, corn muffin mix, green chiles, whole corn, and creamed corn in a large bowl with the onion mixture. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Stir together and pour into the prepared baking pan. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Drop sour cream by the spoonful on top of the mixture; cover with Cheddar cheese. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is firm, about 45 minutes. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
