Ingredients:
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 20 green olives with pimento, chopped
- 1 bunch celery, trimmed and cut into logs

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese and sour cream. Stir in the walnuts and chopped olives. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Spread filling onto the celery pieces. Allrecipes/Qi Ai Cook’s Note To make bite-sized appetizer servings, cut the celery into 1-inch pieces instead of logs. The filling is also good on crackers.
- Serve and enjoy! Allrecipes/Qi Ai
