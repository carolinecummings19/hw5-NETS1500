Ingredients:
- 2 pounds Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 ½-inch pieces
- 5 large garlic cloves, peeled and smashed
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- ⅓ cup unsalted butter, cut into 8 pieces, plus more for serving
- ¾ cup whole milk
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine potatoes, garlic, and 1 tablespoon of the salt in a medium saucepan with enough water to cover by 1 inch. Bring to a boil over high. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until potatoes are fork-tender, 10 to 12 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Drain potatoes and return to hot saucepan. Stir until dry, about 1 minute. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add butter, 1 piece at a time, stirring gently until melted before the next addition. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Using a potato masher, mash in milk until smooth. Stir in 1 teaspoon salt and white pepper. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Transfer potatoes to a serving bowl and top with more butter, if desired. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 6
