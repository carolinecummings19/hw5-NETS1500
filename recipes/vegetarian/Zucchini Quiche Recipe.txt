Ingredients:
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 1 (9-inch) pie shell, unbaked
- 6 large eggs, beaten
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
- Spread grated zucchini on the bottom of the pie shell. Pour beaten eggs over zucchini and top with Cheddar cheese.
- Bake in preheated oven until eggs are set, about 30 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
314 Calories 22g Fat 16g Carbs 14g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 1 (9-inch) quiche
