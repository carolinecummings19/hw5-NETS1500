Ingredients:
- 1 bunch lacinato kale, rinsed and dried
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Â¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- salt to taste

Directions:
- Cut along both sides of kale ribs and discard. Stack kale leaves and slice crosswise into ribbons.
- Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic and crushed red pepper until fragrant, 20 to 30 seconds. Add kale; toss and saute for 3 minutes. Add water and continue cooking until kale is just wilted, 2 to 3 minutes more.
- Drizzle with red wine vinegar and season with salt. Toss and serve.

90 Calories 4g Fat 12g Carbs 4g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 6 mins
Total Time: 16 mins
Servings: 4
