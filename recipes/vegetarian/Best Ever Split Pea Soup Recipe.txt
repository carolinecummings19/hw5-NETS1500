Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cups chopped onion
- 2 cups chopped carrot
- 2 cups finely chopped celery
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 cup yellow split peas
- 1 cup green split peas
- 8 cups fat-free chicken broth
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt-free seasoning blend
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Julian Hensarling / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
- Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, celery, and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Julian Hensarling / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
- Stir in broth, split peas, and seasoning blend; cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring frequently, until split peas are tender, about 2 ½ hours. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Julian Hensarling / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
- Puree the soup with an immersion blender and serve. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Julian Hensarling / Prop Stylist: Julia Bayless
