Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 8 ounces halloumi cheese, sliced into 1/2-inch pieces

Directions:
- Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
- Brush olive oil over both sides of each slice of halloumi cheese.
- Place halloumi directly on the grate and grill for 3 minutes; flip and grill 3 minutes more. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts:
207 Calories 18g Fat 1g Carbs 12g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
