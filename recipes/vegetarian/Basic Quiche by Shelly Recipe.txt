Ingredients:
- 2 cups half-and-half cream
- 4 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 4 ounces Jarlsberg cheese, shredded
- 2 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie shell

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Whisk cream and eggs together in a large bowl. Season with white pepper, nutmeg, and salt. Place shredded Jarlsberg and mozzarella into the pie shell. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour egg mixture over cheese. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake until crust is golden and filling is set, 25 minutes. Allow quiche to set before serving, 5 to 10 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Additional Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 55 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 1 9-inch quiche
