Ingredients:
- 1 cup hot water
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 2 Â¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix hot water, sugar, and shortening in a large bowl. Let cool until lukewarm. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in yeast until dissolved, then mix in flour, egg, and salt. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover and let dough rise until doubled in size, about 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Grease 8 muffin cups. Divide dough equally into the prepared muffin cups. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Let rise again until doubled in size, about 30 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, about 10 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve warm and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
