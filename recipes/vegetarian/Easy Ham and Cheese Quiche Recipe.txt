Ingredients:
- 1 refrigerated pie pastry for 9-inch crust
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
- ½ cup shredded Swiss cheese
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, divided
- ½ cup diced cooked ham
- 2 tablespoons honey mustard
- 1 ¼ cups half-and-half
- 5 large eggs, beaten
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Press pastry into the bottom of a 9-inch pie plate. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes. Poke holes into the pastry with a fork and continue cooking until lightly browned, about 5 minutes more. Remove crust from the oven, and reduce the oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, Swiss cheese, and 1 tablespoon flour in a bowl; spread over warm crust. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix ham and honey mustard together in a bowl; spoon over cheese mixture. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix half-and-half, eggs, green onions, remaining 1 tablespoon flour, and salt together in bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Pour mixture carefully over ham layer; top with remaining Cheddar cheese. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven for 40 to 50 minutes until set in the center. Cool briefly before cutting and serving. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
