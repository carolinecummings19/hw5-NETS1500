Ingredients:
- ¾ cup vegetable oil
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry Ranch-style dressing mix
- 1 ½ teaspoons garlic salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
- 1 ½ (15 ounce) packages mini pretzels

Directions:
- Mix together oil, dressing mix, garlic salt, cayenne, and lemon pepper in a small bowl until combined.
- Place pretzels in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour in ranch dressing mixture. Seal the bag and shake to coat. Allow pretzels to marinate for about 2 hours before serving. Shake occasionally to maintain coating.

Nutrition Facts:
161 Calories 17g Fat 3g Carbs 0g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Additional Time: 2 hrs
Total Time: 2 hrs 5 mins
Servings: 10
