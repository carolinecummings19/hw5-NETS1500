Ingredients:
- 1 (16 ounce) can jackfruit in brine, drained
- 1 cup barbeque sauce
- 2 tablespoons margarine (such as Earth BalanceÂ®)
- 4 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
- Remove all seeds from jackfruit. Combine jackfruit and BBQ sauce in a slow cooker. Cook on High for 3 to 4 hours.
- Use a fork to shred jackfruit meat. Spread margarine on hamburger buns and place a scoop of jackfruit "meat" on top. Serve immediately

Nutrition Facts:
318 Calories 10g Fat 50g Carbs 4g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 3 hrs
Total Time: 3 hrs 10 mins
Servings: 4
Yield: 4 servings
