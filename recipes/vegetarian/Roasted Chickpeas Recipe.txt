Ingredients:
- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon smoked paprika
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- 1⁄8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1⁄8 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (220 degrees C). Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place chickpeas in a bowl and blot with a paper towel to dry them. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Toss chickpeas and olive oil in a bowl. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Season to taste with garlic salt, cayenne, and salt and toss again. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Spread chickpeas on a rimmed baking sheet. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Roast in the preheated oven until browned and crunchy, 30 to 40 minutes; watch carefully to avoid burning them. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
