Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 9 large potatoes, peeled
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (6 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 teaspoon onion salt
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 dash ground black pepper
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 pinch dried parsley flakes, or to taste
- 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain potatoes; reserve water for mashing, if necessary. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Add sour cream, cream cheese, onion salt, garlic powder, and pepper to the potatoes. Using a potato masher or electric beater, beat the potato mixture until creamy. Add reserved cooking liquid, 1 tablespoon at a time, only if needed to achieve a creamy consistency. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Transfer mashed potatoes into the prepared casserole dish; sprinkle Cheddar cheese, parsley, and paprika on top. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 35 minutes. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
