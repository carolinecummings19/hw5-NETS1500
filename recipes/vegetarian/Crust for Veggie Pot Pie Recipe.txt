Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- â…” cup shortening
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 8 tablespoons ice water

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
- Mix together flour, shortening, and salt in a large bowl using a pastry blender. Mix in 6 to 8 tablespoons ice water until mixture forms a ball. Divide ball in half.
- Using a rolling pin, roll 1 ball to fit the bottom and sides of an 11x7-inch baking dish. Roll out the remaining ball to form top crust.
- Use pie crust as directed in your favorite pie recipe.

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 5 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 1 double crust for an 11x7-inch dish
