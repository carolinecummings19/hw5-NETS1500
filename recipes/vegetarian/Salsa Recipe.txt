Ingredients:
- 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 tomatillo, diced (Optional)
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- salt to taste
- 1 jalapeño pepper, minced

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Combine tomatoes, onion, tomatillo, cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and salt in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Mix well. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add 1/2 of the jalapeño pepper and taste. If you desire your salsa with more kick, add remaining 1/2 jalapeño. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Cover the salsa and chill until ready to serve. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
