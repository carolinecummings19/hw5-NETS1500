Ingredients:
- 16 ounces fresh strawberries, hulled and large berries cut in half
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste

Directions:
- Place strawberries in a large bowl. Drizzle vinegar over strawberries, then sprinkle with sugar. Stir gently to combine. Cover and let sit at room temperature for at least 1 hour but no more than 4 hours.
- Grind black pepper over strawberries just before serving.

Nutrition Facts:
60 Calories 0g Fat 15g Carbs 1g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Additional Time: 1 hr
Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins
Servings: 6
