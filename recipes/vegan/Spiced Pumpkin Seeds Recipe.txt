Ingredients:
- 1 ½ tablespoons margarine, melted
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic salt
- 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cups raw whole pumpkin seeds

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
- Combine the margarine, salt, garlic salt, Worcestershire sauce and pumpkin seeds. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Mix thoroughly and place in shallow baking dish. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 1 hr
Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 2 cups
