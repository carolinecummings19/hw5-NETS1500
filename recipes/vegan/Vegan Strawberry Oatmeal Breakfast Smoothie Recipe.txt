Ingredients:
- 1 cup almond milk
- 14 frozen strawberries
- 1 banana, broken into chunks
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 ½ teaspoons agave nectar (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)

Directions:
- Combine almond milk, strawberries, banana, oats, agave nectar, and vanilla extract in a blender; blend until smooth.
