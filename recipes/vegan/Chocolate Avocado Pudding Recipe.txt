Ingredients:
- 2 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
- ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ⅓ cup coconut milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon (Optional)

Directions:
- Blend avocados, cocoa powder, brown sugar, coconut milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a blender until smooth. Transfer pudding to a container with a lid; refrigerate until chilled, about 30 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
412 Calories 27g Fat 42g Carbs 5g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Additional Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 40 mins
Servings: 4
