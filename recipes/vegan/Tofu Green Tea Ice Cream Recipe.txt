Ingredients:
- 1 (12 ounce) package silken soft tofu, drained
- 1 cup unsweetened soy milk
- 1 cup white sugar, or to taste
- Â¼ cup green tea powder (matcha)
- 2 tablespoons canola oil

Directions:
- Place tofu into a blender or the bowl of a food processor; blend until smooth. Add soy milk, sugar, green tea powder, and canola oil; blend until combined.
- Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
