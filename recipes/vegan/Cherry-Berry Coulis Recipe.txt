Ingredients:
- 2 cups frozen mixed berries and cherries
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 dash salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
- Combine fruit, sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat until fruit is thawed and just warmed through, about 5 minutes. Do not boil.
- Transfer fruit mixture to the bowl of a small food processor; pulse until smooth, 15 to 20 seconds.
