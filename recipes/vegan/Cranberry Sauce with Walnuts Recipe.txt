Ingredients:
- 2 (12 ounce) packages fresh cranberries
- 2 cups orange juice
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 oranges, zested
- 1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
- Combine cranberries, orange juice, sugar, and orange zest in a large saucepan over medium heat. Bring to boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
- Stir in walnuts until incorporated. Refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight before serving.

Nutrition Facts:
289 Calories 8g Fat 56g Carbs 3g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Additional Time: 8 hrs
Total Time: 8 hrs 25 mins
Servings: 10
