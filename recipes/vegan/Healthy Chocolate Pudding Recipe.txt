Ingredients:
- 1 cup almond milk
- ¼ cup coconut sugar
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 1 ½ tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
- Heat almond milk, coconut sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and vanilla extract in a saucepan over low heat; cook and stir until thick and smooth, about 10 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:
142 Calories 2g Fat 31g Carbs 2g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 2
Yield: 1 cup
