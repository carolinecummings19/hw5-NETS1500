Ingredients:
- ½ pound young dandelion greens, washed and torn
- ½ red onion, chopped
- 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Toss dandelion greens, red onion, and tomatoes together in a medium bowl. Season with basil, salt, and pepper.
