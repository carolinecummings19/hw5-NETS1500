Ingredients:
- 8 apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- ½ cup water
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:

- Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
