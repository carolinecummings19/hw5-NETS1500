Ingredients:
- ¼ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon coarse salt or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add olive oil, salt, and pepper; cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Add asparagus and cook until fork-tender, turning asparagus often to ensure even cooking, about 8 to 10 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
