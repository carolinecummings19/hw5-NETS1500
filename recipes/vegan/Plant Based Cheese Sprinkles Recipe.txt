Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup cashews
- 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Place cashews in a food processor. Pulse until tiny crumbs form. Remove the lid and add the nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt. Pulse just a few more times until combined, and mixture has a crumb texture but is not yet a powder.
