Ingredients:
- 12 whole fresh mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Clean mushrooms with a damp paper towel; carefully break off stems. Chop stems extremely fine, discarding the tough end of stems. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and chopped mushroom stems; fry until any moisture has evaporated, taking care not to burn garlic. Set aside to cool. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Stir in cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne. The mixture will be very thick. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Use a teaspoon to fill each mushroom cap with a generous amount of stuffing. Arrange mushroom caps on the prepared cookie sheet. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Bake in the preheated oven until the mushrooms are piping hot, about 20 minutes. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Serve and enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 25 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 12
Yield: 12 stuffed mushrooms
