Ingredients:
- 1 cup coconut oil, melted
- 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 cup maple syrup

Directions:
- Mix coconut oil, cocoa powder, and maple syrup together in a bowl until evenly combined.

Nutrition Facts:
121 Calories 10g Fat 11g Carbs 1g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 5 mins
Servings: 24
Yield: 3 cups
