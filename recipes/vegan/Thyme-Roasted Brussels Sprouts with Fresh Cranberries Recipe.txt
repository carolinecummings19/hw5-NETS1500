Ingredients:
- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh thyme
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup fresh cranberries
- 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
- Toss Brussels sprouts in a bowl with olive oil, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Transfer to the prepared baking sheet in an even layer.
- Cook in the preheated oven for 10 minutes; stir in cranberries. Continue cooking for 10 minutes more.
- Transfer sprouts and cranberries to a serving bowl; drizzle with maple syrup and toss to coat.
