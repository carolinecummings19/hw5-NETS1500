Ingredients:
- 1 cup frozen strawberries, slightly thawed and chopped
- 1 frozen banana, chopped
- ½ cup unsweetened almond milk
- ½ cup ice
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro, leaves and stems, lightly packed
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- ½ teaspoon stevia powder (Optional)

Directions:
- Combine strawberries, banana, almond milk, ice, cilantro, lime juice, and stevia powder in a blender; blend until smooth, adding more almond milk or water for a smoother consistency.

Nutrition Facts:
222 Calories 2g Fat 55g Carbs 3g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 1
