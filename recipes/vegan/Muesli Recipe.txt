Ingredients:
- 4 ½ cups rolled oats
- 1 cup raisins
- ½ cup toasted wheat germ
- ½ cup wheat bran
- ½ cup oat bran
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar, or to taste
- ¼ cup raw sunflower seeds

Directions:
- Combine oats, raisins, wheat germ, wheat bran, oat bran, walnuts, brown sugar, and sunflower seeds in a large bowl; mix well. Store muesli in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 months.

Nutrition Facts:
188 Calories 6g Fat 32g Carbs 6g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 16
Yield: 8 cups
