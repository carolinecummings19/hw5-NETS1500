Ingredients:
- 1 cup oats
- 1 cup almond-coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons shredded coconut
- ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
- Combine oats, almond-coconut milk, chia seeds, coconut, cardamom, cinnamon, vanilla, ginger, and nutmeg in a serving bowl until well combined. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.

Nutrition Facts:
267 Calories 10g Fat 37g Carbs 8g Protein 

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 10 mins
Additional Time: 8 hrs
Total Time: 8 hrs 10 mins
Servings: 2
