Ingredients:
- 2 large cucumbers, peeled
- ⅓ cup rice vinegar
- 4 teaspoons white sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
- 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out any large seeds. Slice crosswise into very thin slices. Allrecipes/Qi Ai
- Place vinegar, sugar, ginger, and salt in a small bowl; mix well to combine. Add cucumber slices and stir to coat. Allrecipes/Qi Ai

Recipe Details:
Prep Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 5
