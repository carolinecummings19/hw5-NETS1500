Ingredients:
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ⅔ cup water
- ⅓ cup white vinegar
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon ketchup

Directions:
- Gather all ingredients. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Place sugar, water, vinegar, soy sauce, cornstarch, and ketchup in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Stir continuously until mixture has thickened. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
- Enjoy! Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
