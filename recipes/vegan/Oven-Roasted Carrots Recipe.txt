Ingredients:
- 8 ounces carrots, peeled and cut into 3-inch-long pieces
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ¼ cup chopped pecans (Optional)
- ¼ cup panko bread crumbs (Optional)

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
- Toss carrots with olive oil and Italian seasoning in a bowl; transfer to a rimmed baking sheet.
- Roast in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Sprinkle pecans and panko bread crumbs on top; continue roasting until golden, about 5 minutes more. Serve immediately.
