Ingredients:
- 1 fresh pineapple

Directions:
- Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) according to the manufacturer's instructions. Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper.
- Core pineapple using a pineapple corer or slicer; slice into rings. Place pineapple rings into the prepared basket.
- Air-fry until slices start to roast, 8 to 10 minutes. Flip slices; air-fry 3 to 5 minutes more.
