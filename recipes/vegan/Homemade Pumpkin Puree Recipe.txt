Ingredients:
- 1 sugar pumpkin

Directions:
- Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
- Cut pumpkin in half, stem to base. Remove seeds and pulp. Cover each half with foil.
- Bake in the preheated oven, foil-side up, until tender, about 1 hour.
- Scrape pumpkin flesh from shell halves and puree in a blender. Strain to remove any remaining stringy pieces. Use or store in the freezer in freezer-safe bags.
