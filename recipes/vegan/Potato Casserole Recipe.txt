Ingredients:
- 1 (30 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup butter, divided
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 cups crushed cornflakes

Directions:
- Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Lauren Jewel / Prop Stylist: Hannah Greenwood
- Pour hash browns into the prepared dish; set aside. Combine cheese, sour cream, and condensed soup in a large bowl; set aside. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Lauren Jewel / Prop Stylist: Hannah Greenwood
- Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large skillet over medium heat; saut√© onion in hot butter for 5 minutes. Add to cheese mixture and mix until combined. Spread over hash browns in the dish. Sprinkle with cornflakes. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Lauren Jewel / Prop Stylist: Hannah Greenwood
- Melt remaining 1/2 cup butter in the skillet over medium heat and pour over cornflakes. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Lauren Jewel / Prop Stylist: Hannah Greenwood
- Enjoy! Jen Causey / Food Stylist: Lauren Jewel / Prop Stylist: Hannah Greenwood
